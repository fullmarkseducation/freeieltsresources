## The basic shape

Most IELTS Task 2 essays can be handled with four clear paragraphs:

- Introduction with your position
- Body paragraph 1 with your first main idea
- Body paragraph 2 with your second main idea
- Conclusion that answers the question directly

## Why it works

This structure keeps your argument easy to follow. It also helps you avoid spending too much time on planning when you need to write under exam conditions.

## Quick practice

Take one question and write only the topic sentence for each body paragraph. If the topic sentences clearly answer the prompt, the full essay will usually be easier to finish.
