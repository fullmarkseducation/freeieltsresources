## Use a repeatable plan

For most cue cards, prepare your answer in four moves:

- Say what the topic is
- Give one or two details
- Tell a short story or example
- Explain why it matters to you

## Time yourself

Practise speaking for one minute first. Then repeat the same card and stretch the answer to two minutes by adding examples, feelings, and comparisons.

## Review one thing at a time

Do not try to fix every mistake in one recording. Listen again and choose one focus: fluency, grammar, pronunciation, or vocabulary.
