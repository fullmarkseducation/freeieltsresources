If you're studying for the IELTS and plan to use the Reddit community r/IELTS to help you in any meaningful way, I feel sorry for you. 

Overall, the main problem with r/IELTS isn't their lack of tangible information on how to get better, nor is it their unwillingness to offer advice to the people who ask for help (without paying a major fee of course!). The main problem with r/IELTS is that their attitude. Really. Their attitude is so bad that people in the community are left feeling more confused and unsure than when they left.![hestia-hysterical-part-1000000](</uploads/Screenshot 2026-05-20 at 11.34.44 AM.png> "really?")
